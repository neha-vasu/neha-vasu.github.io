Hi lovelies, Neha here!

This website is for human beans who are precious and beautiful but who struggle to see themselves that way.

I honestly doubt myself every single day a lot more than I should. I find myself constantly apologizing for myself even if it's not my fault or if it's not a big deal. I wonder if I low-key think of myself as a burden.

Although encouragement from others helps, in the end it's up to you to love yourself.

Because you are beautiful. You are enough.
